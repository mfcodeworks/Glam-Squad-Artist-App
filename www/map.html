QR Pay

Booked an event outside of Glam Squad? Have your client open their Glam Squad app and scan the above QR code to use QR pay.

QR pay was designed to make payment easily for your freelance bookings. No bank transfers, cash in hand or any hassle needed.

After your client scans the QR code above they can enter the amount they need to transfer you and the money will be transferred directly to your Stripe account, the same as a regular Glam Squad booking!
